    Inspired by the beauty of Mother Nature, our Branches wallpaper recreates the essence of early spring, with a latticework of grey foliage set against an dove white, snow heavy sky. Lovely in its simple and unassuming intricacy, its clever marriage of purity and attention to detail creates a timeless elegance that will lift pared-back modern interiors without overwhelming them.
